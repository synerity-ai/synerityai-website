Cookie Policy

This cookie policy explains how Synerity Pvt. Ltd. (“Synerity”, “we”, “our”) uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help us deliver a secure, reliable, and personalized experience.

How we use cookies

  • Essential cookies: enable core site functionality such as form submissions.
  • Analytics cookies: help us understand how visitors interact with the website so we can improve it.
  • Preference cookies: remember your settings to provide a consistent experience.

Managing cookies

You can adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent. Please note that disabling cookies may impact certain features of the website.

Third-party cookies

We may use trusted third-party services for analytics or marketing. These providers may set their own cookies subject to their privacy policies.

Updates

We may update this policy periodically. The latest version will always be available on this page along with the effective date of the updates.
